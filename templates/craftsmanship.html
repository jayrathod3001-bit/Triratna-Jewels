The Master's Touch

SCULPTING

WE USE A COMBINATION OF TRADITIONAL WAX CARVING AND MODERN PRECISION MOULDING TO CREATE THE ARCHITECTURE OF OUR DESIGNS, ENSURING BOTH DURABILITY AND DELICATE BEAUTY.

SETTING

OUR GEMSTONE SETTERS WORK UNDER HIGH-RESOLUTION MICROSCOPES, ENSURING THAT EVERY PEARL AND DIAMOND IS HELD WITH ABSOLUTE SECURITY AND OPTIMAL LIGHT REFLECTION.

POLISHING

THE FINAL RADIANCE OF A TRIRATNA PIECE IS ACHIEVED THROUGH A MULTI-STAGE HAND-POLISHING PROCESS, GIVING THE METAL A MIRROR-LIKE FINISH THAT WILL LAST A LIFETIME.
